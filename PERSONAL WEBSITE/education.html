Molina Kids Care Learning Center

Molina was the first school I ever studied at. I attended Molina since preschool, as you can see in the graduation picture from when I was in prep. I received many medals throughout my time there, as I was an achiever from a young age. I vividly remember crying when my parents left me at Molina, but fortunately, the teachers were friendly, and I grew to love the school. Even now, the principal recognizes me when I visit since the school is near my house. I'm grateful that Molina became such an integral part of my life.

Nazareth School of National University

NSNU holds a significant place in my heart and life. I practically grew up there, having studied at NSNU from grade 1 to grade 12. Over those 12 years, I witnessed how NSNU evolved. I deeply appreciate the people who surrounded me, especially my friends, as I have wonderful memories with them at this school. As you can see from the picture, I also achieved a lot during my time at NSNU. The photo captures my graduation from grade 12, marking the end of my time there. It was a bittersweet moment because NSNU had become my second home, and leaving was difficult. However, that's what life is all about, we must move on in order to continue growing.

Pamantasan ng Lungsod ng Maynila

PLM is one of my dream schools in college. Since high school, I always told myself that I wanted to study at PLM, and fortunately, I was accepted and given the opportunity to study there. I was ecstatic when PLM accepted me because my family also wanted me to attend that school. Adjusting to PLM was challenging since it was a completely new environment for me after spending 12 years in the same setting as a child. Thankfully, I am gradually adapting to my college life at PLM, thanks to the warm welcome and friendliness of the friends I've made. I truly hope to graduate and thoroughly enjoy my college experience at PLM.
