MY TOP 3 FASHION OUTFITS

1ST OUTFIT

In this photo taken in 2022 during our family trip to Tanay, Rizal, I wanted to showcase my love for thrifted clothing. The pants and long sleeve shirt I'm wearing were thrifted, while the tube top and bag were purchased from Shopee. Then the necklace from Tala by Kyla adds a touch of elegance to the outfit. I adore this ensemble because it effortlessly combines fashion and comfort, while the color coordination creates a clean and appealing look.

2ND OUTFIT

Moving on to the next photo, it was captured last Christmas in 2023. The top was ordered from Shopee, and I found the perfect pair of pants at a thrift store. To accessorize, I chose a necklace and earrings from Mikana, and the rings are from Tala by Kyla. This outfit exudes elegance and can be worn for a casual event. That's precisely why I love it so much.

3RD OUTFIT

Lastly, this picture was also taken in 2022. The inner top and shorts were thrifted, while the cardigan was ordered from Shopee. I complemented the look with a necklace from Tala by Kyla and a bag from Oaklavie. This simple yet stylish outfit made me feel like a fashionable Korean in a summer season. The color scheme, particularly the use of brown, creates a clean and pleasing aesthetic.
